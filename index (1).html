Selected Programs

Enterprise LMS Governance & a Self-Built Feedback Loop
Honu Services
LMS Administrator · Apr 2023 – Present
Problem
An enterprise LMS serving 2,000+ users across multiple clients needed consistent governance, faster support turnaround, and a real channel for user feedback to reach curriculum decisions instead of getting lost across Help Desk, email, and tickets.
Approach
Took end-to-end ownership of the platform — user management, content deployment, reporting, integrations, and system optimization — and restructured support intake to cut response times. Built an LMS Feedback App in PowerApps so user insight could feed continuous improvement in a structured way, and led ongoing curriculum governance reviews for accuracy and consistency.
Outcome
Clients meeting and exceeding 90% compliance completion targets, improved platform reliability and adoption, and a standing feedback mechanism that didn't exist before.

Scaled Blended-Learning Design for a Global Audience
Hyland
L&D Specialist — ID & LMS Admin · Jun 2021 – Apr 2023
Problem
Global audiences needed a steady stream of blended learning — eLearning, ILT, and VILT — that stayed tied to business and performance goals rather than being produced on its own schedule, inside an org running on Agile release cycles.
Approach
Ran needs analysis and design/evaluation strategy per initiative, then built the courses in Articulate 360 and Adobe tools. Embedded course-effectiveness and assessment measures to track impact, supported Workday LMS configuration and data migration, and ran multiple concurrent ID projects using Agile/Scrum alongside product owners, developers, Scrum Masters, and analysts each release.
Outcome
Learning content shipped on-cadence with every release and stayed measurable, not just delivered. Also mentored facilitators on ID standards, templates, and tools, raising design consistency across the team.

Global LMS Rollout & Regulated Compliance Recordkeeping
Dreyer's / Nestlé USA
Training Specialist / LMS Admin · Jan 2011 – Jun 2021
Problem
A regulated manufacturing environment needed a global Cornerstone deployment covering 6,000+ hourly employees, audit-ready GMP documentation, and a way to address production micro-holds tied to training gaps.
Approach
Contributed to the global Cornerstone LMS rollout while independently maintaining training records for 450+ employees. Managed controlled documentation — SOPs, SMPs, specifications, logbooks — inside a document-control system, and designed blended ILT and self-directed learning. As part of the Education & Training Work Group, applied the ADDIE model directly to the micro-hold cost problem, and helped build the Competency Building Plan that developed 75+ certified trainers.
Outcome
Micro-hold costs tied to training gaps reduced from over $700K to $0. 75+ trainers certified. Audit-ready compliance documentation sustained across the site.
